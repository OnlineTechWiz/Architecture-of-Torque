Chapter 1: The Mechanics of Discipline

In both trading and production, most people fail because they mistake "activity" for "progress." They trade every candle. They add a hundred layers to a track that needed one perfect melody.

The Producer’s Oath: "I do not chase. I do not force. I only execute when the structure aligns with my blueprint. If the trade/track isn't ready, neither am I."
